# Dash0 pricing and cost — fundamentals

## Core idea

Dash0 prices on telemetry volume with a deliberately transparent model and no hidden fees, positioning predictable cost as a product feature rather than an afterthought. The model is meant to make spend legible — you pay for the data you send — instead of the opaque per-host or per-feature tiers common in agent-based suites. Agent0 is metered separately on task-based credits, where a task represents a meaningful action such as an investigation, an asset, or a pull request.
