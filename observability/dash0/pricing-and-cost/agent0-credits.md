# Dash0 pricing and cost — Agent0 credits

## Core idea

Agent0's autonomous work is billed on its own meter: task-based credits, separate from the volume-based platform charge. A credit is consumed by a meaningful task — an investigation, a generated asset, or an opened pull request — so AI cost maps to delivered outcomes rather than conversation length. Keeping the agent on a distinct meter lets teams reason about telemetry spend and automation spend independently.
