# Dash0 pricing and cost — volume-based model

## Core idea

The platform charges on telemetry volume — the amount of trace, metric, and log data ingested — rather than per host, per user, or per feature. Tying cost to volume makes spend predictable and directly attributable: more data costs more, and you can lower the bill by sampling or trimming what you send. This contrasts with agent-based suites whose per-host or tiered pricing can grow opaquely as a fleet scales.
