# Dash0 Agent0 — automated assets and PRs

## Core idea

Agent0 creates production-ready solutions conversationally rather than stopping at advice. It generates and opens pull requests with fixes, builds dashboards and alert rules from natural-language descriptions, and produces instrumentation guides for new services. Turning an investigation directly into reviewable assets is what moves it from a diagnostic tool to one that does the follow-up work a human engineer would otherwise have to.
