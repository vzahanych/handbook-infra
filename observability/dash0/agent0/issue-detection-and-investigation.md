# Dash0 Agent0 — issue detection and investigation

## Core idea

Agent0 continuously monitors across all services and signals to identify problems before they reach customers, then investigates rather than merely flagging. It goes beyond symptom detection to uncover root causes by analyzing traces, logs, and metrics together, the way an on-call engineer would triage. Pairing always-on detection with autonomous investigation is what lets it compress the slow first phase of an incident — noticing and forming a hypothesis.
