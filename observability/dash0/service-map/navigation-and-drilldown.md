# Dash0 service map — navigation and drill-down

## Core idea

The map doubles as a navigation surface: a node or edge is the entry point into the traces, logs, and metrics behind it. Starting from the topology lets you spot where trouble concentrates and then drop straight into the evidence for that specific service or dependency. This turns the map from a static picture into the top of an investigation funnel that ends in a concrete span or log line.
