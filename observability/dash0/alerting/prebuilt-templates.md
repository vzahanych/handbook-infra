# Dash0 alerting — pre-built templates

## Core idea

Dash0 ships access to over 400 pre-built alert templates drawn from open-source knowledge bases, so common conditions come ready-made. Reusing community-curated rules means you start from proven thresholds for databases, runtimes, and infrastructure instead of authoring every alert from scratch. Because they are standard PromQL, the templates also coexist with your own existing Prometheus rules rather than replacing them.
