# Dash0 alerting — notification channels

## Core idea

When a check fires, Dash0 routes the notification through integrations such as email, Slack, and other channels so the right people learn of it where they already work. Delivery is the last mile of alerting: a correct check is useless if its signal never reaches a responder. Routing to existing tools, rather than a separate inbox, is what keeps alerts inside the team's normal workflow.
