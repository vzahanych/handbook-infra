# Dash0 dashboards — variables and templates

## Core idea

Variables and pre-made templates make a dashboard reusable across services and environments rather than hardcoded to one. A variable parameterizes a dashboard — pick a service, namespace, or environment and the panels repoint — so one definition serves many targets. Templates give a tested starting point, so a new service inherits a sensible view instead of being built from a blank canvas.
