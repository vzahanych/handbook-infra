# Dash0 log management — search and filter

## Core idea

Log management centers on fast search and filtering, letting you narrow millions of lines to the relevant ones by attribute, severity, time, and free-text. The goal is instant exploration — questions answered as you type rather than after a long query — so investigation keeps pace with an incident. Filtering on standardized attributes is what makes results precise instead of guesswork over raw strings.
