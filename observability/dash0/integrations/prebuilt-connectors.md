# Dash0 integrations — pre-built connectors

## Core idea

A pre-built connector packages the configuration needed to bring a specific source online — the right OpenTelemetry receiver or instrumentation, plus curated dashboards and alerts for that source. Instead of researching which signals matter and wiring them by hand, you pick the integration and get a working, opinionated setup. This is the "instant observability" promise: the catalog absorbs the boilerplate so onboarding a new technology is a selection, not a project.
